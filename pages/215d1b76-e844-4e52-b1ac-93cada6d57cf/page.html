Where we accumulate data about Switzerland in order to relocate there.

What is the objective?

The objective is to know enough about Switzerland in order to relocate there.

How is Switzerland governed?

The Confederation has certain competences provided by the Constitution, such as currency or the army, while the cantons have all competences not attributed, such as police, education, or health.

— Unknown, Wikipedia

What is Switzerland HDI?

Switzerland's HDI is the highest in the world.

Is Switzerland considered a free economy?

Switzerland is one of the top countries in terms of economic freedom.

Is digitalisation important?

There is a digitalization initiative for the entire Swiss business federation since at least 2020. There is still significant digitalization work to be done in the vast majority of companies along the following lines:

  • Artificial intelligence
  • Cybersecurity
  • Blockchain
  • Digital transformation management

Switzerland ensures its prosperity sustainably and seizes the opportunities offered by digitalization

— Federal Council, Objectives of the Federal Council 2024

Switzerland consistently applies the "digital first" principle to benefit everyone, regardless of gender, age, or origin. It takes advantage of digital transformation so that everyone benefits sustainably. Switzerland is among the most competitive and innovative European countries in terms of digitalization.

— Swiss Confederation, Swiss Digital Strategy

To whom is digitalisation important?

Digitalisation is important mostly for companies with a turnover over $100MM.

Digital technologies are significantly more important for large companies than for small and medium-sized Swiss enterprises.

While one in two companies with an annual turnover of more than 100 million francs attaches great importance to digital technologies, this rate is only 20 percent for very small companies.

32 percent of service providers, 30 percent of industrial companies, and 22 percent of companies in the construction and energy sectors attach great importance to digital technologies for their business models.

— Le Monde Économique

Where to find information about companies?

Registries of Commerce of Switzerland. Each canton has its own database. Unfortunately, there is no database that would allow filtering companies by types of activities, turnover, location etc.

Which cantons are the most economically active?

According to Federal Statistical Office, the most economically active cantons are:

  1. Bâle-Ville
  2. Zoug
  3. Genève
  4. Zurich
  5. Neuchâtel
