Once this material is learned, obtaining the B2 German certificate should be more likely. The Socratic Method is used.

The objective is to reach proficiency in Standard German, in the most efficient way possible.
  • A list of questions and answers of increasing complexity is available here: QA.
  • In essence, it follows the Socratic Method.
  • The complexity progression is inspired from The Little Schemer

One hour of study means:

  1. 15min: Review last lessons.
  2. > 10 new words a day.
  3. For all sentences: read, listen, speak, write, explain.
