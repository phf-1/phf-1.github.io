Context

The objectives pursued in order, bottom to top. An objective may be tagged with a keyword.keyword is ommitted to prevent noise.

  1. Means « yet to be done ».
  2. Means « a result is being built ».
  3. Means « waiting for an other actor response ».
  4. Means « a result has been built and it satisfies the objective ».
  5. Means « a result — if any — has been built and it does not satisfy the objective ».
