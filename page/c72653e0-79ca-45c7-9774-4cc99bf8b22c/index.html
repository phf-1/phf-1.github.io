How do actors communicate?

Actors interact with each other only by one actor sending another actor a messenger (message), which is also an actor. Message and messenger are completely interchangeable notions.

What is the difference between OOP and the Actor Model?

OOP models behaviour and state, whereas Actor Model models (pardon a tautology) computation and is a precise, complete, formally defined theory as opposed to OOP.
