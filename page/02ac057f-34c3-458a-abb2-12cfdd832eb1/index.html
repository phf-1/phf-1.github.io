Reading a research paper

  1. Read the Abstract (and optionally Introduction) (2-10 minutes).
  2. Read the Conclusion, then skim most of the paper (10-30 minutes).
  3. Thoroughly read the entire paper, with the goal of being able to understand the intricate details (30 minutes - 3+ hours)

Research paper structure

Building a research paper

  • Identify a problem
  • Review related work
  • Offer a solution to the problem
  • Defend the proposed solution
